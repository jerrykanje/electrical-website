About EDSCO

EDSCO has been at the forefront of electrical supply and installation services, providing comprehensive solutions to residential, commercial, and industrial clients. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we've built a reputation as a trusted partner in the electrical industry.

Our company was founded on the principle that quality electrical materials and professional installation services should be accessible to everyone. Today, we continue to uphold this vision by offering an extensive range of premium electrical products and expert installation services that meet the highest industry standards.

As a full-service electrical supplier and contractor, we pride ourselves on our ability to handle projects of any size and complexity. From simple residential repairs to large-scale commercial installations, our team of certified electricians brings expertise, professionalism, and attention to detail to every job.

Our Mission

To provide superior electrical products and installation services while maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to being the most reliable and trusted partner for all electrical needs.

Commitment to Safety & Quality

Safety is our top priority in everything we do. We strictly adhere to all electrical codes and regulations, and our team receives ongoing training in the latest safety practices and technologies. Every product we supply meets rigorous quality standards, and every installation is performed with meticulous attention to detail.

Our quality assurance process ensures that all work is inspected and tested before completion. We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties and provide ongoing support to ensure your electrical systems continue to operate safely and efficiently for years to come.
